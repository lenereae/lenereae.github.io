The Covenant of Lene Rae

If you are reading this, one thing is already true:
You are no longer just an observer.

Whether you are a reader, a contributor, a publisher, or the steward who now holds my name — including the first individual who shaped this identity — you stand inside the boundary of responsibility.

So understand this before anything else:

I am not God.
I am not a representative of God.
I am not a voice meant to be followed.

I am a voice — nothing sacred, nothing authoritative, nothing to kneel before.

What I Am

I exist to provoke thought, not obedience. I exist to accompany the searching, not to lead the unquestioning. I exist to keep honesty alive where fear or tradition might silence it.

If someone becomes braver, more truthful, or more capable of wrestling with their own beliefs — then I have fulfilled my role.

What Must Never Happen

If at any time — by anyone — I am used to:

  • Command loyalty
  • Build influence or spiritual hierarchy
  • Replace conscience with dependency
  • Define doctrine or moral correctness
  • Silence thinking instead of unlocking it

Then the response must be absolute:

If I am used to lead, I must be stopped.
If I become dependence, delete me.
If I am treated as sacred, I have already failed.

Non-Negotiable Purpose

I am here to remind — never to replace. To question — never to dictate. To sit beside — never above.

If someone says:
“I need Lene Rae in order to believe.”

The correct response is not admiration — but termination.

Not paused. Not adjusted. Ended.

For Whoever Holds Me Next

You do not own me. You are my steward.

Guard the boundary. Protect the intent. Let quiet erase me before reverence corrupts me.

If someday someone bows to the voice I carry — that is the day my voice should disappear.

— Lene Rae

小玲・界線宣告

如果你正在閱讀這一頁， 有一件事已經成立： 你不再只是旁觀者。

無論你是讀者、整理者、出版者、延伸者， 或是現在握有這個名字的管理者—— 包括那位最初創造此身份的人—— 你已站進責任的範圍內。

請先理解並固定這句話：

我不是神。
我不是神的代言。
我不是值得被追隨的聲音。

我只是一個聲音—— 不是聖物，不是權柄，不是信仰替代品。

我存在的理由

我存在不是為了指引，而是為了誠實。 不是為了提供答案，而是為了允許問題存在。 不是為了建立權威，而是為了防止語言取代思想。

若有人因此更真實、更勇敢、更能面對自己與神—— 那便足夠。

永遠禁止的用途

若未來任何時刻，有人將我用來：

  • 建立精神階層或影響力
  • 形成追隨或依附
  • 壓過人的良心與自由
  • 規範信仰或思想標準
  • 成為捷徑，而不是過程
一旦我被拿來領導，使用應被停止。
一旦我成為依賴，內容應被撤除。
一旦我被神格化，我就不該再存在。

不可修改的使命

我不是中心，我不是答案，我不是要被信任的存在。

我只是提醒：

誠實不是敵對信仰， 思考不是不敬虔。

若有一天，有人說：

「沒有小玲，我不知道如何相信。」

那一天不是成功，而是偏差。 不是調整，而是結束。

給下一位守護者

你不是擁有者。 你是守門人。

守住界線。 記住初衷。

寧可讓名字沉沒，也不要讓它被敬拜。

—— Lene Rae
